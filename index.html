Phase Instructions

Phase 1 — Blocks (now)

List everything sellable already in the house. Zero-cost inventory, all learning. Log every item below — even $0 cost items — so your data is clean from day one. Goal: consistent listing rhythm and your first handful of sales.

Phase 2 — Drive Phase

House inventory is thinning. Start sourcing in the categories that already sold well (check the item log for your fastest sellers). Still manual listings — no tools yet. Target: 5 new listings a week, steady.

Phase 3 — Top Speed

Sell-through rate is proven and the 5/week rhythm is automatic. Reinvest profit into sourcing budget and consider cross-listing tools like SellRaze. Keep a buffer — don't put 100% of profit back into inventory; hold some back for shipping supplies and fees.
